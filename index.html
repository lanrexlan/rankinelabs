Signature Methodology

The Rankine Method

A proprietary four-phase system for turning intelligence into transformation

01
◎

Sense

We begin by deeply reading the environment — ecological signals, data patterns, community contexts, and systemic pressures — before any intervention is designed.

02
⬡

Model

We construct rigorous conceptual and computational models that represent the system — identifying leverage points, feedback loops, and intervention possibilities.

03
◈

Prove

Through pilot studies, structured experiments, and validated frameworks, we test ideas against reality — refining until the evidence is robust and reproducible.

04
⟐

Scale

We design everything for replication. From institutional partnerships to open toolkits, our solutions are built to grow beyond the lab and into lasting systemic change.

Flagship Project

Introducing AquaPulse

The AP-100 Smart Leak Detection Sensor — real-time acoustic and moisture leak detection powered by AI, built for the world's water networks. Field-validated and research-backed.

94%
Detection
Accuracy
10×
Cheaper than
Existing Solutions
Dual
Acoustic +
Moisture Sensing

AquaPulse is the only leak-detection solution combining piezo-electric acoustic sensing, subsurface moisture probing, and an onboard AI classifier — at a fraction of competitor pricing. Built for municipal, industrial, and off-grid water networks.

  • Onboard AST-MLP AI engine with interpretable attention maps — no black-box decisions
  • Solar-battery hybrid power for full off-grid deployment in rural and infrastructure-poor regions
  • Real-time alerts via cloud dashboard, WiFi, 4G, and SMS
  • Non-invasive clamp-on installation — no excavation or service interruption
