How Research Becomes Action

A concise process for moving from inquiry to practical implementation.

01

Research

We investigate system constraints, opportunities, and context-specific evidence.

02

Frameworks

Findings are translated into usable decision frameworks and implementation guides.

03

Prototypes / Concepts

We shape practical tools and concept models for testing and iteration.

04

Applied Partnerships

With partners, concepts are adapted for institutional and community application.

Concepts in Development

Status: Concept

BoreholeIQ

Domain: Water infrastructure intelligence
Problem: Boreholes are often managed reactively and fail without warning
Approach: Low-cost sensor monitoring with predictive failure analytics
Potential users: Estates, schools, hospitals, churches, private operators

Status: Concept

AquaCheck

Domain: Water quality intelligence
Problem: Many communities consume untested water
Approach: Portable testing, contamination mapping, and treatment recommendations
Potential users: Households, small labs, NGOs, community health actors

Status: Concept

FloodSense Nigeria

Domain: Flood risk intelligence
Problem: Recurring flooding with limited early warning
Approach: Geospatial and rainfall data prediction with community alerts
Potential users: Community leaders, emergency agencies, NGOs, local authorities

Status: Concept

PureFlow

Domain: Decentralized water treatment
Problem: Unsafe or expensive rural water access
Approach: Solar-powered modular treatment micro-stations with remote monitoring
Potential users: Community operators, rural entrepreneurs, local institutions

Status: Concept

HydroScout

Domain: Water access intelligence
Problem: Limited mapping of informal water infrastructure
Approach: Satellite imagery, OpenStreetMap, drone surveys, and community data collection
Potential users: Governments, development agencies, NGOs, planning institutions

Principles

Human-centred AI

Intelligent systems should improve judgment, transparency, and lived outcomes.

Evidence-led decisions

Methods, claims, and recommendations remain grounded in verifiable evidence.

Sustainability by design

Resilience, environmental stewardship, and long-term system value are built in.
