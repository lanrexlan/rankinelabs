Concepts in Development

Status: Concept

BoreholeIQ

Domain: Water infrastructure intelligence
Problem: Boreholes are often managed reactively and fail without warning
Approach: Low-cost sensor monitoring with predictive failure analytics for private borehole systems
Potential users: Estates, schools, hospitals, churches, private facility operators

Status: Concept

AquaCheck

Domain: Water quality intelligence
Problem: Many households and communities consume untested water
Approach: Portable water testing with a mobile logging app, contamination mapping, and treatment recommendations
Potential users: Households, small labs, NGOs, community health actors

Status: Concept

FloodSense Nigeria

Domain: Flood risk intelligence
Problem: Communities face recurring flooding with limited early warning
Approach: Flood prediction using public geospatial, rainfall, elevation, and river data, with alerts delivered to communities
Potential users: Community leaders, emergency agencies, NGOs, local authorities

Status: Concept

PureFlow

Domain: Decentralized water treatment
Problem: Rural communities face unsafe or expensive water access
Approach: Solar-powered modular water treatment micro-stations with remote monitoring and health tracking
Potential users: Community operators, rural entrepreneurs, local institutions

Status: Concept

HydroScout

Domain: Water access intelligence
Problem: Reliable mapping of informal water infrastructure is limited
Approach: Satellite imagery, OpenStreetMap, drone surveys, and community data collection to build water infrastructure intelligence
Potential users: Governments, development agencies, NGOs, planning institutions
